Emerald Ash Bore

Information about this bug

The Emerald Ash Bore was discovered in 2002, in Michigan, and has spread all throughout the United States, it kills trees and is considered a pest. It has killed hundreds of millions of ash trees in North America, and it caused regulatory agencies to stop the bugs in infested trees.

How to get rid of Emerald Ash Bores

Well there aren't that many options, the only thing that works right now is to cut down the tree that they are in.
